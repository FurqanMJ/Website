Lotus Temple

The Lotus Temple in New Delhi is not only an architectural marvel but also a symbol of unity. A mind-blowing fact about the temple is that it has no idols, altars, or religious symbols, welcoming people of *all faiths and beliefs* to meditate and reflect in silence. Its unique design, inspired by the lotus flower, features **27 free-standing marble "petals"** arranged in clusters of three, creating nine sides—a structure that appears to float like a blooming lotus. Even more fascinating, this Bahá'í House of Worship is one of the most visited buildings in the world, often surpassing iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Taj Mahal in annual footfall!
