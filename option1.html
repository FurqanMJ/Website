Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is not just an architectural wonder but also a symbol of impeccable precision. One mind-blowing fact is that the four minarets surrounding the Taj Mahal are slightly tilted outward. This was an ingenious design choice to ensure that in the event of an earthquake or structural collapse, the minarets would fall away from the main dome, protecting it from damage. This subtle engineering marvel showcases the brilliance of Mughal architecture.
