Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Karnataka, India, was once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire and is filled with architectural marvels. A mind-blowing fact is that the *stone chariot* in the Vittala Temple complex, which appears to have wheels, was designed so realistically that they could once rotate. Although now fixed to preserve it, the craftsmanship remains unparalleled. A unique fact about Hampi is its musical pillars at the Vittala Temple.
